About Me
It All Started When...

My name is Rasha Corpuz, 19 years old, born in 2005. I currently live in Montalban, Rizal. I graduated from the Asian Institute of Computer Studies – Montalban, where I completed my senior high school education under the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand. I started filming in 8th grade, but my passion for it grew in 11th grade. It’s a way for me to express my creativity freely, and I feel genuinely happy when people recognize and appreciate my work. I also enjoy video editing—it’s the most thrilling and exciting part for me because it pushes my creativity to the limit. I feel the same way about graphic design, where I can unleash my creativity and design visuals in an original way.
